ClawCost vs Helicone

ClawCost blocks overspend before it hits your card. Helicone shows you what already happened. Here's the full breakdown.

ClawCost

Local-first cost control

Hard budget enforcement that blocks requests before charges occur. All data stays on your machine in SQLite. Free to self-host, five-minute setup. Best when privacy and preventing surprise bills are the priority.

Helicone

Cloud observability platform

Full-featured LLM observability routed through Helicone's cloud. Strong team collaboration, prompt management, and analytics. Best when you need a shared observability layer across a team and don't mind cloud routing.
